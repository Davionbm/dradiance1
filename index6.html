YOUR STORY AND GOD (3)

When God is in your story, you attract the attention of Satan!

In part one and two, we talked about different ways that Satan craftily steps in to derail the plan and purpose of God in a man’s life. Any plan of God in your life always attracts the attention of Satan, and some of the ways he does that are DESIRE and FEAR (PLEASE CHECK PART ONE AND TWO)

JOSHUA THE HIGH PRIEST A CASE STUDY

In this part, we are looking at the life of Joshua the high priest.

ACCUSATION – This is one of the ways Satan tries to intervene in the story of God for your life. He brings in all kinds of accusations in order to impede, derail or obstruct the plan and purpose of God.

“Then he showed me Joshua the high priest standing before the angel of the LORD, and Satan standing at his right side to accuse him.” Zechariah 3:1 (NIV)

What are the accusations that Satan always accuses you of?

1. Wrongdoing
2. Disobedience
3. Sin

Regarding your future, Satan creates fear. Regarding your past, he brings accusations.

Satan accuses you of wrongdoing by stirring up people around you to dwell on something you did wrong in the past. But in regard to sin and disobedience, he always accuses you before the Heavenly Father of your sin and disobedience to God. But God has forgiven you by putting a new garment of forgiveness on you – “The angel of the LORD said to Satan, “May the LORD condemn you, Satan! May the LORD, who loves Jerusalem, condemn you. This man is like a stick snatched from the fire.” Joshua was standing there, wearing filthy clothes. The angel said to his heavenly attendants, “Take away the filthy clothes this man is wearing.” Then he said to Joshua, “I have taken away your sin and will give you new clothes to wear.” Zechariah 3:2-4 (GNT)

So don’t allow Satan to imprison your mind with guilt of your past. Repent and move on, and if you have repented, then rejoice in God’s forgiveness and live well
